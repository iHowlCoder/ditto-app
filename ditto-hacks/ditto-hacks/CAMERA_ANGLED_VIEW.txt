OLD CAMERA POSITION
----------------------------------------------------------------

Position: SCNVector3(0, 0, 15)
  X: 0 (centered horizontally)
  Y: 0 (centered vertically)
  Z: 15 (15 units back)

View: Straight-on, head-on view

  - No depth perception
  - Flat 2D appearance


NEW CAMERA POSITION
----------------------------------------------------------------

Position: SCNVector3(3, 2, 25)
  X: 3 (shifted right)
  Y: 2 (shifted up)
  Z: 25 (much farther back)

View: Angled 3D perspective
  - Looking down at model from above-right
  - Better depth perception
  - Shows 3D structure clearly
  - More cinematic angle

Use these to find the perfect camera angle!


CODE STRUCTURE
----------------------------------------------------------------

// Position camera at angle
cameraNode.position = SCNVector3(3, 2, 25)

// Look directly at model origin
cameraNode.look(at: SCNVector3(0, 0, 0))

The .look(at:) method ensures the camera is always
pointing at the DNA model, no matter where it's positioned.
